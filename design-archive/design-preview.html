Era Primer

The HGSS format is defined by Pokémon-Prime powerhouses and the "Call for Family" mechanic on many basics. Consistency is everything — draw supporters like Cleffa (HS) and Sage's Training are format staples. Eelektrik / Magnezone remains the top tier archetype; Gothitelle ACE SPEC lock is the premier control strategy. The lack of VS Seeker makes Junk Arm the critical recycling card, and it shows in every competitive list.
